02

A Problem That's Growing

Detections have increased 23× since 2008. More testing reveals more impaired drivers on our roads.

Scale of Testing

500,000+ tests conducted annually. 55,391 total detections since 2008—that's 151 impaired drivers caught per day in 2024.

Testing Disparity

NSW and VIC conduct most tests. Smaller jurisdictions like NT show higher detection rates relative to population.

Cocaine Surge

Cocaine detections up 133% in 2024. Amphetamine remains the most commonly detected substance overall.
