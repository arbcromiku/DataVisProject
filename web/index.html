Police Enforcement - Positive Drug Tests (2008-2024)

Data visualization project for COS30045 Data Visualisation

Research Questions

  1. Age Patterns: How do drug test rates vary across age groups, and which drugs are most prevalent in each age demographic?
  2. Geographic Trends: How have drug test rates evolved over time across Australian jurisdictions, and are there regional patterns in drug type prevalence?
  3. Drug Correlations: What correlations exist between different drug types in positive tests, and how do these patterns vary by age and location?
  4. Enforcement Analysis: What's the relationship between positive drug tests and enforcement outcomes (fines, arrests, charges), and how do these vary by drug type and jurisdiction?
  5. Detection Impact: How do different detection methods affect accuracy and outcomes of drug testing, and what are the implications for understanding true drug usage patterns?
